SUMMARY OF DELEGATED COMMITMENT AUTHORITY
This document records the rules and guidelines approved by the Business Council and other Board-delegated committees to govern external commitments to partners and customers for core business, as defined by the Commitment & Signature Authority Policy
Note: This document covers delegated commitment authority for Google Inc. Delegations and escalations for Other Bets are documented in go/ob-delegations
Note
: Unless otherwise stated at time of initial approval, Google Deal Review approvals are valid for six months. All deals that have not been executed six months after the Deal Review approval must be re-escalated to validate that non-standard terms are still acceptable. Material changes or amendments to any deals previously reviewed at a Google Deal Review must be re-escalated to that Deal Review. Where applicable, commitment authority is granted within established budgets; where incremental budget is required to satisify the requirements of a deal, commercial commitment authority is insufficient to secure it. Instead, incremental budget must be approved through established annual, quarterly or ad hoc business planning processes.
