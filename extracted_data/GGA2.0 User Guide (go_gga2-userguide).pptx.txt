CSAT Survey
CSAT survey are sent via email to deal initiators after a deal has been reviewed and an outcome has been recorded.
The survey will include questions about the forum attended, review date, approval type, and whether the process was beneficial. There will also be an optional free-response question.
After completing the survey, users will see a confirmation window. 
The survey will be sent one business day after the deal outcome is recorded, and CC'd to forum advisors. 
A dashboard has been created to display the results.
