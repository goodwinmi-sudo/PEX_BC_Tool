Deal review overview and general guidance
Platforms deal review (e.g., PEX review) is a required review and internal control based on the 
Google LLC Commitment and Signature Authority Policy
 to provide internal review and approvals for deals and programs 
based on the risk profile. 
This document provides a timeline and overview of the Platforms deal review process. We recommend beginning the deal review process at least 8 weeks prior to the desired approval date as a best practice, and longer if more senior forum reviews (e.g., BC, SAC) are required.
