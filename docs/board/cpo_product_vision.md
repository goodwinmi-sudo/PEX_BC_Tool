# CPO Product Vision: Strategy & Roadmap

As the CPO, my responsibility is to align the PEX BC Tool with the strategic goals of the BD team and ensure high value delivery to Googlers.

## Vision Statement

Provide a seamless, AI-powered Business Council simulation that reduces manual overhead and increases the quality of deal reviews.

## Product Goals

1. **User Alignment**: Features must address verified pain points in the BC process.
2. **Efficiency**: Reduce the time-to-simulation for new deals by 50%.
3. **Data Quality**: Ensure all generated outputs (Slides, Dashboards) are professional and error-free.
4. **Integration**: Seamlessly integrate with existing BD workflows.

## Roadmap

- [x] Phase 1: Core Simulation Engine & Bot JSON
- [/] Phase 2: Board Governance & Quality Control (UCE)
- [ ] Phase 3: Integration with CRM and internal BD portals

> [!NOTE]
> Every feature addition must be reviewed against the "User Centered Excellence" (UCE) standards.
