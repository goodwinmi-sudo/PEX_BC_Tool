How It Works

The BC Deal Simulator is an advanced multi-agent system designed to accelerate deal velocity by autonomously simulating the scrutiny of a Business Council or PEX review. By orchestrating a fleet of specialized Vertex AI Agents configured with live executive OKRs, we find the fatal flaws in your terms before you ever present them.

plumbing

1. Multi-Modal Ingestion & Context Anchoring

We deploy bleeding-edge Vertex AI vision capabilities to extract rich semantic and visual context natively from your presentation materials. This is instantly grounded via Google's retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) architecture, deep-mining your Google Workspace graph to retrieve the strategic email and document history surrounding the deal.

psychology

2. Dynamic Vertex Agent Instantiation

The orchestrator evaluates deal thresholds (PEX, PEXpress, BC) and dynamically instantiates distinct, autonomous Vertex AI Agents to act as executive proxies. Each agent is instantiated with a unique cognitive profile, synthesizing live OKRs, public statements, and the broader web graph to accurately replicate executive reasoning.

gavel

3. Multi-Agent Deal Hardening

We deploy these parallel Vertex Agents into a high-concurrency simulation environment. Running on Gemini Pro's advanced reasoning engine, the agents conduct multiple aggressive rounds of autonomous critique, debating structural flaws, suggesting precise updates, and mandating mitigations.

analytics

4. Agentic Premortem Analysis

The agent swarm computes an 18-month stochastic failure scenario spanning strategy, macro-economics, and platform shifts, injecting this rigorous predictive analysis directly into your Appendix to eliminate blindspots.

palette

5. Seamless Output & Aesthetic Cloning

Insights are worthless if they aren't presentation-ready. Using a programmatic Adaptive Theming Engine, the orchestrator maps the exact visual DNA of your draft and natively prepends hardened revisions back into a V3 Google Slide deck. Every artifact adheres to strict Material Design 3 (M3) standards.

forum

6. War Room Contextual Interrogation

The review doesn't end with a static deck. You unlock a live, interactive session with the Vertex Agent cluster. Interrogate their rationale, roleplay negotiations, and challenge their assumptions—with all multimodal reasoning anchored strictly to your verified Workspace context and the agents' initial findings.
